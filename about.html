About Cyklze

Sell Your Scrap Easily with Cyklze!

Do you have old paper, plastic, metal, e-waste, or cardboard lying around your home, office, supermarket, or business? With Cyklze, turning your scrap into cash has never been easier. Simply book a pickup, and we’ll take care of the rest.

How It Works

  • Book a Pickup: Open the app, check current prices, select pickup type, choose a convenient time slot, add your items, and confirm your address.
  • We Collect & Weigh: Our team arrives at your location, collects your scrap, and weighs it in front of you for complete transparency.
  • Get Paid: Receive instant payment via cash or UPI—fast and hassle-free.

Who Can Use Cyklze?

Currently serving Hyderabad (with plans to expand), Cyklze is perfect for:

  • Homeowners: Declutter and sell old papers, bottles, and electronics.
  • Businesses: Easily dispose of office scrap and maintain a clean workspace.
  • Factories & Construction Sites: Sell accumulated metal, plastic, and other scrap materials.

What We Buy

  • Paper: Newspapers, magazines, books, office paper
  • Plastic: Bottles, containers, packaging
  • Metals: Aluminum, steel, iron, copper
  • E-Waste: Phones, laptops, chargers, cables
  • Other Items: Glass bottles, cardboard

Why Choose Cyklze?

  • Convenient doorstep pickup—no need to visit a scrap dealer.
  • Transparent weighing—everything happens in front of you.

Information We Collect

  • Phone Number: For login and communication
  • Recyclables Info: Type, estimated weight, pickup date & time
  • Address: For scrap collection

Your privacy is important to us. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more details.
