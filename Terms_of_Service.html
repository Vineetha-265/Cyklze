3. Payment-Related Information

Payment Method:

Cyklze provides a scrap pickup service where users can book a pickup for their scrap and receive payment for the collected scrap. The amount paid will be determined based on the weight and type of scrap, as well as the price per kilogram, which is visible in the application and locked at the time of booking. Payments will be made in cash at the time of pickup.

Booking a Pickup:

When you book a scrap pickup through the Cyklze platform, you will be required to:

  • Select the pickup type (e.g., Mass, Supermarket, General, ASAP)
  • Enter the estimated weight of the scrap
  • Select recyclable types (e.g., metal, plastic, paper)
  • Pick a date and time for the pickup
  • View the price per kilogram (locked at the time of booking)

Payment Calculation:

Once you confirm your pickup details, Cyklze will arrive on the scheduled date to collect your scrap. The scrap will be weighed on-site, and the amount payable will be based on the actual weight and the locked price per kilogram from the day of booking. The final payment will be given to you in cash at the time of the pickup.
